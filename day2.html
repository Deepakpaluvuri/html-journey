The vandhe Bharat Express launched in 2019

>Vande Bharat Express, also known as Train 18, is a semi-high-speed train developed by the Indian Railways. It is designed to provide a comfortable and efficient travel experience for passengers. The train features modern amenities, including air-conditioned coaches, onboard entertainment, and advanced safety features. Vande Bharat Express is known for its speed and efficiency, making it a popular choice for travelers in India.

The Brand New BMW f 450 gs

The BMW F 450 GS is a new entry-level adventure motorcycle from BMW Motorrad, designed to replace the G 310 GS. It features a 420 cc liquid-cooled, parallel-twin engine producing 48 hp at 8,750 rpm and 32 lb-ft (43 Nm) of torque at 6,750 rpm, mated to a 6-speed manual transmission with a bidirectional quickshifter (standard on higher trims)
